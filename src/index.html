Battleship

How to play

  1. There is two 20 x 5 grid on this game. Bottom one represents your map , and the other ona represents the enemy
  2. The main point is to shot all of the enemy ships that are hiding in enemy area
  3. You can see your own ships on the map. Your ships are green ones.
  4. You can't see your enemy's ships. So you must guess when you are shooting.
  5. When your shoot hits the enemy ship, the square will be displayed in red
  6. When you hit all parts of a ship, the ship will sink and display in dark red
  7. Your damaged and sunk ships will take color related to their situation as well as enemy's ships
  8. If you manage to destroy all of the enemy ships , you will win. Otherwise you will lost.
